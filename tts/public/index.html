ℹ️ About

This Text-to-Speech app uses your browser's built-in speech synthesis capabilities. No server required!

  • Voice: Choose from available system voices
  • Rate: Control speaking speed (0.5x to 2x)
  • Pitch: Adjust voice pitch (0 to 2)
  • Volume: Set audio volume (0% to 100%)

💡 Tip: For the best experience, use Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.
